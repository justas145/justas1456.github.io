What is Chia Network?

A new blockchain, which is easy to use, more efficient and secure. It uses Proof of Space and Time, reducing energy consumption significantly compared to Proof of Work. Chia mining is based on the amount of hard disk storage space devoted to it. As Chia Network is becoming more popular, an increasing amount of exchanges are listing Chia (Exchanges - ChiaLinks)

How The Plotting Works?

Sending Plots Through The Internet

  • Tell us your pool public key and farmer public key (totally safe, as these keys are public).
    ‍
  • Tell us how many plots you want.
    ‍
  • After we are done plotting we will send a google drive link, where you can download the plots from (you can also send your google drive link to us).
    ‍
  • We can send around 8-10 plots per day.

Plotting Into The Hard Drive

  • Tell us your pool public key and farmer public key (totally safe, as these keys are public).
    ‍
  • Tell us how many plots you want.
    ‍

How long does it take to download a plot?

It depends on your internet speed. One plot has 101.4 GiB (108.8 GB), so the time it takes to download a plot is equal to 108.8 GB / your Internet speed in MBps.

Can we plot into the internal hard drive?

Yes we can, however the external hard drives are cheaper and there is no difference where you are storing your plots.

How much power does the farming uses?

The farming barely uses any power, but as a rule of thumb, for one TB it is around 1-2 W.
